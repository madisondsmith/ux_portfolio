A Song Bird's Obligation

By Madison Smith

What obligation does a bird have to sing today?
Does a bird sing because it is simply happy to?
Because it has wings today?
The opinion is –
Of the Jay –
One might say:
“It sings to pray!”
“Pray a mate might fly its way!”
But if the Jay is naturally inclined to sing today,
He might say:
“I simply have a song today.”
“–today so I’ll sing today.”
His mate may simply flock his way –
Having heard his song today
His way – as naturally as he did say
That he had a song today
Wasting not his time to fly this way –
This way nor to yonder fray
Then afterward, he will sing today? No.
Wasting not his time idly
dreaming to sing.
One might say:
“He sings same as he flaps his wings!”
“–his wings which flap before one has chance to say, ‘he sings same as he flaps his wings!’“
So I say:
“Be not idle if you want to sing!
Sing your song.
If it is good,
Life may simply flock your way.”
