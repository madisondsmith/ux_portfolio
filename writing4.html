A Song Bird's Obligation

By Madison Smith

What obligation does a bird have to sing today? Does a bird sing because it is simply happy to? Because it has wings today? The opinion is Of the blue jay One might say: “It sings to pray” “Pray a mate might fly it’s way” But if the jay is naturally inclined to sing today He might say: “I simply have a song today” “Today so I’ll sing today” His mate may simply flock his way Having heard his song today His way as naturally as he did say That he had a song today Wasting not his time to fly this way This way nor to yonder fray Then afterward, he will sing today, no. Wasting not his time idly dreaming to sing One might say: “He sings same as he flaps his wings” “His wings which flap before one has chance to say, ‘he sings same as he flaps his wings’ “ So I say: “Be not idle if you want to sing sing your song If it is good Life may simply flock your way”
