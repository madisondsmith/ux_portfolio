Belong

By Madison Smith

Somewhere
Something
Bigger than you
Belong
With it all
You must earn it
It cannot be attained
It cannot be tamed
it cannot be bought
It must be given
Bestowed upon
You might lose something
What have you gained?
What did you do?
Where did you come from?
Did you not belong there?
Will you remain?
No
It was a vail
A shroud
Easy to wear
Though heavy
It is difficult to remove
Though not impossible
It is the necessary struggle
Do not despair
Press on
The sprout pushing through dirt
Out of dark
It belongs
