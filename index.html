As a UX Designer, I use creativity, service, critical thinking, and empathy as the medium by which the experiences of others are translated.

What shapes a community is a person. What shapes a person is what they see and how they interact with what they see. It is, therefore, vital that our communities be virtuously and ethically designed.

The vocation of humankind is that of a gardener. We must tend our surroundings with no notion of ownership but rather of something borrowed.

This is my aim as a Designer.
This is my aim as a human being.
